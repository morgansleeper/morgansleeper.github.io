Morgan Sleeper

Hi! I'm a Ph.D. Candidate in Linguistics at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My primary research interests are music and language, the description of Patagonian Welsh, and Language in Japanese popular media. I'm currently working on my dissertation, which describes new methodologies for the integration of musical and linguistic analyses in structural linguistics, sociocultural linguistics, and language revitalization.
